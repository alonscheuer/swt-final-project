label: 2022 FIFA World Cup knockout stage
comment: The knockout stage of the 2022 FIFA World Cup is the second and final stage of the competition, following the group stage. Played from 3 to 18 December, the knockout stage will end with the final, held at Lusail Iconic Stadium in Lusail. The top two teams from each group advanced to the knockout stage to compete in a single-elimination tournament. There are sixteen matches in the knockout stage, including a third place play-off played between the two losing teams of the semi-finals.
