abstract: The 2023 AFC Asian Cup will be the 18th edition of the AFC Asian Cup, the quadrennial international men's football championship of Asia organised by the Asian Football Confederation (AFC). The tournament will involve 24 national teams after expansion in 2019. The tournament was originally scheduled to be held in China from 16 June to 16 July 2023. On 14 May 2022, the AFC announced that China would not host the tournament due to the circumstances caused by the COVID-19 pandemic and China's Zero-COVID policy. On 17 October 2022, the AFC announced that the tournament will be held in Qatar. Due to the high summer temperatures in the Gulf region, the tournament will probably be postponed from mid-2023 to early 2024.
