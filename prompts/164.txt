It was the first edition of the Miami Grand Prix and the fifth round of the 2022 Formula One World Championship. The race was won by Max Verstappen, ahead of championship leader and pole-sitter Charles Leclerc in second and his teammate Carlos Sainz Jr. in third. Verstappen also set the fastest lap.
