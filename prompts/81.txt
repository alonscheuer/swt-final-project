comment: Group B of UEFA Euro 2024 qualifying is one of the ten groups to decide which teams will qualify for the UEFA Euro 2024 final tournament in Germany. Group B consists of five teams: France, Gibraltar, Greece, the Netherlands and the Republic of Ireland. The teams will play against each other home-and-away in a round-robin format. The top two teams will qualify directly for the final tournament. The participants of the qualifying play-offs will be decided based on their performance in the 2022–23 UEFA Nations League.
