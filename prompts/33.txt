	label: Germany at the 2022 Winter Olympics
comment: Germany competed at the 2022 Winter Olympics in Beijing, China, from 4 to 20 February 2022. Bobsledder Francesco Friedrich and speed skater Claudia Pechstein were the country's flagbearers during the opening ceremony. Meanwhile bobsledder Thorsten Margis was the country's flagbearer during the closing ceremony. Germany finished in the second place at these Olympics. Overall, the nation won 12 gold and 27 total medals which is slightly lower than 14 gold and 31 overall in 2018.
