abstract: The 2023 Masters (officially the 2023 Cazoo Masters) is a professional non-ranking snooker tournament set to take place between 8 and 15 January 2023 at Alexandra Palace in London, England. It will be the 49th staging of the Masters tournament, which was first held in 1975, and the second of three Triple Crown events in the 2022–23 snooker season, following the 2022 UK Championship and preceding the 2023 World Snooker Championship. Broadcast by the BBC and Eurosport in Europe, it will be sponsored by car retailer Cazoo. The defending champion is Neil Robertson, who defeated Barry Hawkins in the 2022 final 10–4.
