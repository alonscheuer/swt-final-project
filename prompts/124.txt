abstract: The 2023 UEFA Super Cup will be the 48th edition of the UEFA Super Cup, an annual football match organised by UEFA and contested by the reigning champions of the top two European club competitions, the UEFA Champions League and the UEFA Europa League. The match will feature the winners of the 2022–23 UEFA Champions League and the 2022–23 UEFA Europa League. It is currently scheduled to be played at the Ak Bars Arena in Kazan, Russia, on 16 August 2023. However, the venue is uncertain due to the 2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine, and UEFA is considering alternative venues. Real Madrid are the defending champions.
